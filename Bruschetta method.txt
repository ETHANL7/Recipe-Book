

1. Boil 2 quarts of water. Once the water is boiling remove from the pot.
2. Put the tomatoes in hot water and blanche for 1 minute.
3. Preheat the oven to 230.
4. Chop the tomatoes, toss them with garlic, olive oil, vinegar, basil, salt and pepper.
5. Use a knife to slice the baguette on the diagonal making half-inch thick slices & brush one side of each slice with olive oil.
6. Place the slices in the oven at 230\'b0 and leave to roast for 5 to 6 minutes.
7. Use a spoon to gently top each toasted bread slice with some of the tomato mixture.
