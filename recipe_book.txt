Ricotta Gnocchi Recipe

Ingredients needed:

Gnocchi:
1 (8 ounce) container ricotta cheese;
2 eggs;
1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese;
1 teaspoon salt;
1 teaspoon pepper;
1 teaspoon garlic powder;
1 cup all-purpose flour;

Sauce:
3 tablespoons olive oil;
1 tablespoon minced garlic;
1 (15.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes;
1 dash crushed red pepper flakes (optional);
6 basil leaves, finely shredded;
Salt and pepper to taste;
8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, cut into small chunks;

Method:
1.Stir together the ricotta cheese, eggs, Parmesan Cheese, salt, pepper, and garlic powder in a large bowl until evenly combined. 
2.Mix in 1 cup of flour. 
3.Add additional flour if needed to form a soft dough. 
4.Divide the dough into 3 or 4 pieces, and roll into 1/2-inch-thick ropes on a floured surface. 
5.Cut each rope into 1-inch pieces, and place on a lightly floured baking sheet. 
6.Place in the refrigerator until ready to use. 
7.Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat. 
8.Stir in garlic, and cook until softened and fragrant, about 1 minute. 
9.Pour in diced tomatoes and red pepper flakes; bring to a simmer over medium-high heat, and cook for 10 minutes. 
10.Stir in shredded basil and season to taste with salt and pepper. 
11.While sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil over high heat. 
12.Boil the gnocchi until they float to the surface, 1 to 2 minutes, then drain. 
13.To assemble the dish, stir the cubed mozzarella cheese into the sauce and allow the heat of the sauce to soften, but not melt the cheese. 
14.Place gnocchi into a serving bowl, and spoon sauce overtop. 




Eggplant Parmesan Recipe

Ingredients needed:

3 eggplants, peeled and thinly sliced;
2 eggs, beaten;
4 cups Italian seasoned bread crumbs;
6 cups spaghetti sauce, divided;
1 (460 grams) package mozzarella cheese, shredded and divided;
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided;
1/2 teaspoon dried basil;


Method:
1.Preheat oven to 175 degrees C. 
2.Dip eggplant slices in egg, then in bread crumbs. 
3.Place in a single layer on a baking sheet. 
4.Bake in preheated oven for 5 minutes on each side. 
5.In a 23x30 cm baking dish spread spaghetti sauce to cover the bottom. 
6.Place a layer of eggplant slices in the sauce. 
7.Sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses. 
8.Repeat with remaining ingredients, ending with the cheeses. 
9.Sprinkle basil on top. 
10.Bake in preheated oven for 35 minutes, or until golden brown. 




Tradition Style Vegan Shepherd's Pie Recipe

Ingredients needed:

Mashed potato layer:
5 russet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch cubes;
1/2 cup vegan mayonnaise;
1/2 cup soy milk;
1/4 cup olive oil;
3 tablespoons vegan cream cheese;
2 teaspoons salt.

Bottom layer:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil;
1 large yellow onion, chopped;
2 carrots, chopped;
3 stalks celery, chopped;
1/2 cup frozen peas;
1 tomato, chopped;
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning;
1 clove garlic, minced;
1 pinch ground black pepper;
1 (400 grams) package vegetarian ground beef substitute;
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar-style soy cheese.

Method:
1.Place the potatoes in a pot, cover with cold water, and bring to a boil over medium-high heat. 
2.Turn the heat to medium-low, and boil the potatoes until tender, about 25 minutes; drain. 
3.Stir the vegan mayonnaise, soy milk, olive oil, vegan cream cheese, and salt into the potatoes, and mash with a potato masher until smooth and fluffy. 
4.Set the potatoes aside. 
5.Preheat oven to 200 degrees C, and spray a 2-quart baking dish with cooking spray. 
6.Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat, and cook and stir the onion, carrots, celery, frozen peas, and tomato until softened, about 10 minutes. 
7.Stir in the Italian seasoning, garlic, and pepper. 
8.Reduce the heat to medium-low, and crumble the vegetarian ground beef substitute into the skillet with the vegetables. 
9.Cook and stir, breaking up the meat substitute, until the mixture is hot, about 5 minutes. 
10.Spread the vegetarian meat substitute mixture into the bottom of the baking dish, and top with the mashed potatoes, smoothing them into an even layer. 
11.Sprinkle the potatoes with the shredded soy cheese. 
12.Bake in the preheated oven until the cheese is melted and slightly browned and the casserole is hot, about 20 minutes.
